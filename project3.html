King Garden" Gaming 3D Environment.

Modeling Gaming Environment, using Blender and Unity.

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing a captivating gaming environment featuring a picturesque garden and a house nestled on a mountain. The environment includes a beautifully landscaped garden, a serene lake, and various other elements designed to create an immersive and aesthetically pleasing experience.

Key Features

  • Detailed Terrain Modeling:
    The landscape was sculpted in Blender to create a realistic garden, mountain, and surrounding environment, serving as the foundation for the gaming world.
  • Intricate House Design:
    A house was modeled with attention to detail to complement the natural surroundings and serve as a focal point within the game.
  • Rigging for Animation:
    Custom scripts were used to randomly generate and place trees, rocks, and other objects, adding to the environment's authenticity.
  • Dynamic Animation:
    A camera movement controller script was developed to capture video within the environment, allowing for fluid and dynamic camera angles.

Process and Development

The development process followed these steps:

  • Conceptualization: The idea of a serene and immersive gaming environment was conceptualized, focusing on a picturesque garden and mountain setting.
  • Sculpting and Modeling:The landscape was sculpted in Blender, followed by the creation of the house, trees, rocks, and other environmental objects.
  • Unity Integration:The models were imported into Unity, where they were positioned within the environment. Custom scripts were written to automate the placement of trees and other objects.
  • Coding: Scripts were developed to control camera movements and enhance the overall gameplay experience.
